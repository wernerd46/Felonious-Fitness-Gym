Why Felonious?

We provide you a state of the art, secure, clean, comfortable atmosphere with the ultimate convenience; 24 hour, 7 day a week access via a card pog locking system. Exercise on your time, anytime.

Unlike most fitness clubs, Felonious Fitness provides you with complete access to the facility 365 days a year. We are never closed. Additionally, we staff the facility Monday through Friday 10:00am - 8:00pm and 10:00am - 2:00pm on Saturday for your membership needs. We also provide a membership with access to a complete list of professional trainers that you can optionally choose to utilize to help you meet your specific goals. If you prefer, you may even utilize your own personal trainer here at our facility. Ask the manager for details.
