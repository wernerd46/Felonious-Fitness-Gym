Jeff Storch

Jeff is an accomplished fitness trainer with over 20 years of industry experience. Jeff has consistently helped his clients achieve their health and fitness goals. A familiar face and friend in the competitive physique sports industry, Jeff is well known and respected for his extensive knowledge, experience, and achievements (his own as well as his clients). As both a long time NPC judge and competitor himself, he knows what the judges are looking for. His engaging rapport makes fitness fun for everyone and has earned him years of dedication and loyalty from many long term clients.
